About

I'm a 20 year old Computer Science student at SUNY Albany. I'm from Brooklyn, New York but originally born in the Dominican Republic. My ultimate goal is to work on significant software, applications, websites, databases, etc. that impact our world!

I love all types of music, dancing, photography, and of course video games. I'm a part of various different organizations on campus from the Student Government as the Information Technology Director, the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE), Association of Latino Professionals For America (ALPFA), Fuerza Latina.

Take a look at the various different projects I've been a part of inidividually, as a free lancer and as an intern here.

Work

Currently I'm interning for the New York State Office of Information Technology Services. There I've been learning an abundance about the software development cycle as a Quality Assurance Tester for the Quality Management Center. I'm using software solutions like HP Unified Functional Testing, HP Application Lifecycle Management, and Katalon Studio.


I'm also the Director of Information Technology for the university's Student Association. I'm the first director of IT ever so it has been challenging but I'm keeping the current site up to date in terms of all branches of the student government.
