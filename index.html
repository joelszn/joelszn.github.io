About Me

Born in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic and immigrated to the United States at age 3. I spent several years living in between countries which helped me develop both English and Spanish.

I attended P-TECH the decorated 6 year early college high school in Brooklyn once visited by former president Barack Obama himself. The school gave students like me from underrepresented backgrounds a chance to attain a college degree. There is where I found my love for technology and was able to develop and receive my Associates of Applied Sciences in Computer Information Systems at the sole age of 18.

Currently I am attending SUNY, University at Albany where I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Business. In my short tenure here since transferring in Fall 2016 I've been able to make deans list join multiple organizations and currently work for Library Systems as a Student Assistant.

I'm extremely interested in entrepreneurship and have dwindled with multiple ideas and look forward to making an impact to our society.
